Goblin Witchdoctor 

Feats
level-1 feat: crafter (herbalists tools, alchemists tools, poisoner's kit) 
level-1 feat: magic-initiate (cantrips: primal savagery, thorn whip, shillelagh, magic stone
                              level 1: dissonant whispers, entangle) 
level-4 feat: ritual caster
 - INT+1 or WIS+1 or CHA+1
 - 2 level-1 ritual spells (always prepared) 
 - can cast ritual w/regular casting time w/o spell slot (1x per spell per long rest) 
level-4 feat: observant
 - INT+1 or WIS+1
 - proficiency in insight/investigation/perception (expertise if you're already proficient)
 - search as a bonus action
level-4 feat: keen mind
level-4 feat: inspiring leader 
 - WIS+1 or CHA+1 
 - give up to 6 allies 2d4+PB THP at end of short or long rest


Fighter-1 
 - weapon mastery (3): slings (slow), blowgun (vex), quarterstaff (topple) 
 - fighting style: blindfighting = blindsight within 10ft

Race: Goblin 
 - size: small 
 - darkvision (60ft) 
 - fey ancestry = adv. on saves against charmed 
 - fury of the small = +PB damage on damage when hitting a creature larger than you (PBx/LR)
 - nimble escape = BA disengage or hide 

Point-Buy Stats 
 - STR = 8 
 - DEX = 14
 - CON = 12
 - INT = 15 (+2) = 17
 - WIS = 14 (+1) = 15
 - CHA = 8

Trickery Cleric 
 - blessing of the trickster (lvl-3) 
   + advantage on stealth check for 1h 
 - domain spells (lvl-3) 
   + charm person, disguise self, invisibility, pass without trace 
 - invoke duplicity (lvl-3) 
   + create illusion as a BA (teleport to location w/in 30ft of it when you create it) 
   + can cast spells from illusion's space 
   + have adv. on attack rolls while illusion is w/in 5ft of creature that can see it 
   + teleport: can move illusion up to 30ft as a BA and then teleport to it's location (swap) 
 - domain spells (lvl-5) 
   + hypnotic pattern, nondetection
 - trickster's magic (lvl-6) 
   + can cast illusion spells as a BA (provided original casting time is an action) 
   + use this feature WISx/long rest 

Illusionist 
 - illusion savant (lvl-3)
   + get 2 new illusion spells from wizard list at level 3 (add to spellbook) 
   + add 1 new illusion spell to spellbook every time you gain access to new wizard spell slots 
 - improved illusion (lvl-3) 
   + know minor illusion - can do sound and image and can cast as BA
   + can cast illusion spells w/o verbal components
   + any illusion spell with range of at least 10ft has range increased by 60ft
 - phantasmal creatures (lvl-6)
   + learn summon beast and summon fey (always have prepared) 
   + can cast these as normal, or can cast them as illusion spells w/o expending a spell slot (free 1x/day)
     but illusory creature has 1/2 the hit points as normal 
 - illusory self (lvl-10) 
   + create illusory duplicate of yourself in reaction to an attack hitting you, causing it to 
     automatically miss (1x/short rest) 
   + triggered by you being hit, rather than just an attack roll being made at you 
   + can restore feature by expending a lvl-2 or higher spell slot
